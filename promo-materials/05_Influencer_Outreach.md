# 微网红合作话术模板

## 模板1：留学生博主（小红书/微博）

```
Hi [博主名]，

关注你很久了，特别喜欢你分享的[具体内容，如：
「在伦敦的生活日常」]。

我是阿拉丁许愿灯 APP 的创始人。这是一个让留学生
之间互相实现愿望的小产品——

比如：
- 一个中国女生许愿「希望有人教我做红烧肉」
  → 3天后收到陌生人的详细菜谱
- 一个在巴黎的女生生日那天一个人过
  → 一个法国陌生人录了生日歌给她

目前完全免费，想把这份善意传递下去。

想邀请你免费体验一下，如果觉得有意思，
希望能分享给粉丝～

不用付费推广，只要真实体验就好 🪔

有兴趣的话，我给你发体验链接！
```

## 模板2：情感/正能量博主（TikTok/Instagram）

```
Hi [博主名]，

Your content always makes me believe in the goodness of 
people. That's exactly why I built this app.

It's called "Aladdin's Wish Lamp" — strangers fulfill 
each other's wishes.

Some examples:
- Someone sang happy birthday to a stranger in Kenya
- A photo of Northern Lights was taken for someone 
  who may never see it in person
- A recipe was taught across 3 time zones

I'd love to have you experience it and share with your 
audience if you think it's worthy.

No sponsorship, no payment — just spreading kindness.

Want to try? 🪔

[链接]
```

## 模板3：小而美播客（英文）

```
Hi [播客主],

I'm a solo founder who built a wish-fulfilling app for 
strangers around the world. 

The story behind it: I was once helped by a stranger 
online, and wanted to build something that scales that 
feeling.

It's called "Aladdin's Wish Lamp" — and it's 100% 
free, no ads, no tracking.

I'd love to come on your podcast and share:
- Why I built it
- The most beautiful wishes that have been fulfilled
- Why I think the internet can be a force for good

Would you be open to having me? Happy to do it for free 
in exchange for the conversation 🙂

Best,
[你的名字]
```
